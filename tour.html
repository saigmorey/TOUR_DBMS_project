Manali

Manali, a magnetic town nestled in the Himalayas, beckons to the soul of adventurers, nature lovers, and solace seekers. Stretched out leisurely on the banks of the River Beas, this charming hill station is blessed with a magnificent scenery characterised by forests, valleys, streams, rivers, and orchards that has increased its popularity faster than the clouds that dance on its hilltops. Explore Manali on foot, or try paragliding over its rolling green slopes and apple orchards. Wonderfully combining nature and adventure, it is serenity, history, nature, spirituality, and shopping, all merged into one. A potpourri of cultures and experiences, Manali is the perfect mountain getaway for everyone.
